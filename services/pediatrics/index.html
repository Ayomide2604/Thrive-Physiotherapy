Pediatric Physiotherapy

At Thrive Home Physio, our team of highly skilled pediatric physiotherapists specializes in treating babies, children, and young adults (0-19 years old). We combine expert clinical care with a fun and engaging approach, ensuring a positive experience for both the child and their parents.

Our Approach:

  • Individualized assessments tailored to each child’s needs
  • Goal-setting in collaboration with parents, carers, and children
  • Personalized treatment plans to improve mobility, coordination, balance, strength, and independence
  • Hands-on therapy including stretches, massage, soft tissue mobilization, and postural advice
  • Carer and parent training to support progress at home

Conditions We Treat:

  • Musculoskeletal & Sports Injuries – Back and joint pain, postural problems, soft tissue injuries, gait abnormalities, hypermobility
  • Developmental Conditions – Developmental delay
  • Respiratory Conditions – Cystic fibrosis, long-term ventilated children
  • Orthopedic Conditions – Fractures, positional talipes, Erb’s palsy, torticollis, plagiocephaly, Perthes disease, pre/post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Neurological Conditions – Genetic syndromes, cerebral palsy, head injuries, and more

Why Choose Thrive Home Physio?

  • Specialist therapists with extensive pediatric experience
  • Engaging therapy sessions designed to make treatment fun
  • Home-based care for maximum comfort and convenience
  • Comprehensive support for children and their families
